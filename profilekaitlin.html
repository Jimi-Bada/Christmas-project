Kaitlin graduated from the University of Bristol with a BSc in Economics and Philosophy. She loved Behavioral Economics, and evaluating economic models. After graduating she went to rural Thailand to teach English and travel, and loved the freedom but wanted to challenge herself more.

Her love for data analytics stemmed from completing work experience as a data analyst at the Department for Education, looking at ways to measure attainment. From there, she started looking into R and python for data visualization, and eventually found The Information Lab, which seemed like a natural next step in her data journey.

Outside of work, she loves playing piano and cello, and for exercise enjoys going to the gym and runs.
