Sherina graduated from the University of Nottingham in 2016 with a degree in Business Management. After graduation, she went on to open two companies; one in luxury travel and one in import and export in Hong Kong. During the pandemic, Sherina discovered data after studying Data Science and Machine Learning in Python and found her long lost passion. She moved to London to kickstart her career in Data Analytics.

Sherina now aims to combine her creative capabilities with her analytical mindset as a data consultant. When introduced to Tableau, she was hooked by its user interface and simplicity of presenting data in a creative and effortless way.  Outside of work, Sherina participates in cricket and has been playing the sport since she was 12 years old, briefly to represent Hong Kong.
