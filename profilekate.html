Kate is originally from Ireland, where she graduated from Maynooth University with a Bachelors of Social Science Degree. During her time in Uni, Kate was introduced to basic Data Analytics skills like SPSS and this peaked her interest in Data. After graduating, Kate worked in the Irish Healthcare Service (HSE) and shortly moved her way into Public Health. Here she worked in the Surveillance of Infectious Diseases with focus on Data Entry, Data Analysis and Data Cleansing..

After searching on different job sites, Kate came across The Information Lab and knew she had found the job for her!

She applied to the Data School shortly before moving to London and is a part of DS37. In her free time Kate loves playing and listening to music. She also enjoys watching football and cooking.
